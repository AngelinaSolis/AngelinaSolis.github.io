Raquelly

What can I say, this not so little girl of mine has always been at my side since day one. She steps up and takes care of her sisters when ever I am not home. She gave me purpose and I am so proud of who she is becoming as a young adult. She has started her own buisness creating personalized cups, t-shirts and holiday ornaments. This year she will graduating high school and her goal is to become a ultra sound technician. Although the first part of her life has been rough she inspires and encourages me to be a better mother.
