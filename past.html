The Dark Ages

I grew up feeling like dirt, you know the stuff people walk on. I was not allowed a voice, I was not allowed to think for myself. I was controlled and told what do to and when to do it. On the outside everything looked normal but on the inside I was dying.

I turned to all different types of drugs to cope & before I knew it, I was in my mid 30's a drug addict, a single parent, and just out of a very mentally, physically and emotionally abusive relationship.

I hit rock bottom hard and fast & if it was not for my children I would have never found my way out.
