Welcome to the Moments of My Life

I graciously welcome you into the journey of what I call, "My Moments of My Life."

12 years ago I would have never thought I would be where I am today. The life I lived and the life I now live are on two very different spectrums. Today I live knowing I have hope and have been set free from all that was meant to destroy me. I am unashamed of who I was because the person I am today has learned and grew from those past mistakes. I am first and foremost a Child of God, I am a mother, a daughter, a sister, a aunt, a student and the sole provider and protector of my family. I have come from the darkest corners of the world and overcome them. My Faith in Christ carries me through all the hardships that this life has to offer.
