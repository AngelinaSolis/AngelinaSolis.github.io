His Word Gave Me a New Life

"Do not comform to the pattern of this world but be transformed by the renewing of your mind. Then you will be able to test and approve what God's will is - his good, pleasing, perfect will. Romans 12:2"

Living In Peace that only He can provide

Growing up in chaos, I did not know that when I become adult I had the choice to leave it behind. I was so used it I didn't think a sober, peaceful life was something I could actually have. The moment I decided to let go and let God take control, the life I once knew was no longer. I found a peace not just within but all around me. Not saying that I am perfect or that I always do or say the right thing, that's impossible because I am a sinner. However I changed and continue to change with each passing day, I care about others, I want the best for others, and I want everyone to know what a Good God we have. Just having the opportunity to create this page instead of being dead or in prison, says more than words could ever express.
