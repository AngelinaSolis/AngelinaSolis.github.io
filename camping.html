Camping life

Camping life is amazing, camping life on/near a beach is heaven. I love the smell of campfire and when my girls and I camp we are at peace with each other, there's no fighting, arguing. My girls work like a team in sync and help with setting up and tearing down. Something about sleeping in the outdoors changes them into sweet loving sisters. We've love to camp with other families and play games, enjoy each other's company and leave the electronics behind. Sleep in tents and the longest we have stayed is a week. That's about all they can take before they get tired of being outdoors.
