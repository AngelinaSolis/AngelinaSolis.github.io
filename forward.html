Keep On Moving

Knowing where I come from, I have no where else to look but forward. As for this moment in time, I do the best I can. I can make plans all I want but the truth is I never know where I am going to end up. Life changes within a blink of an eye and I have no control. I go with the flow, follow the path that is before me. I cut the ubt out and just "Do".
