Life is Too Short to be Wasted Gotta Keep On Moving

I am simple and like simple things, I get happy over the smallest things. For instance the smell of coffee can brighten up a gloomy day. I love walking in the rain, sitting on a beach or driving up into the mountains. Life can get so complicated and stressful sometimes the only thing anyone can do is just smile and keep moving.

It has not always been like this for me. I used to be filled with so much darkness that I really didn't see a way out. The choas was so overwhelming that I hated who I was and pretty much lived life just going through the motions.
